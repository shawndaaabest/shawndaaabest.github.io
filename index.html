About the service

Hi! My name is Martina and I do at home pet sitting and dog walking. The reasoning behind doing at home sitting: it keeps the pet in their natural and comfortable environment; in the comfort of their own home. Our pets already find it stressful that we are not around, so putting them in another environment can be quite unsettling for them.

More Information

How it works

A list is made by you, including the pets' daily schedule, bad habits or things to care for. I follow these on a daily basis or for as long as you are gone for, that way, nothing changes for the pet and they are comfortable and happy. Every pet differs on how comfortable they are with someone so it may take multiple visits till they are fully comfortable.

Note: A meeting needs to be held beforehand.
